ABOUT

Data are transforming our world, and as one of Bangladesh's leading educational institutions, Department of Software Engineering of Daffodil International University aspires to pave the road toward a data-driven smart Bangladesh. With this purpose in mind, we conduct a Data Science Summit every year where we try to bring together leaders and professionals from various areas to engage in active dialogues and share their provocative ideas.

As a part of our ongoing endeavors, this year, the 2nd DIU Data Science Summit 2021, organized by Data Science Lab of the Department of Software Engineering of Daffodil International University, will be a daylong event, taking place on Saturday 11th December 2021 at the Daffodil Smart City, Ashulia.
